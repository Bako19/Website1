ABOUT

The Hyper Tigerz is a collection of 666 Genesis Tigerz descending from the jungle to hunt their $PREY on the Ethereum Blockchain.

ROADMAP

  • Hunting Game

    Let the hunting begin, few days after the launch you can stake your Hyper Tigerz NFTs and send them to hunt to earn our native token $PREY.

    While hunting, each genesis Hyper Tigerz earns 5 $PREY per day, whereas on the other hand Legendary Hyper Tigerz earn 15 $PREY per day.

  • $PREY TOKEN

    $PREY will be an ERC20 Token. You will receive $PREY by hunting, holding your Hyper Tigerz, and through various community rewards.

    1 $PREY = 1 $PREY

  • TIGERZ ARCADE

    Users will be able to play mini-games by buying the arcade tickets using $PREY from the marketplace.

    Climb the leaderboard to claim your $PREY rewards.

  • Gen 2 Launch

    Genesis Hyper Tigerz NFT holders will be able to mint 1 free Gen 2 for each genesis tiger you hold.

    Utility of Gen 2 will be revealed in roadmap 2.0

  • COMMUNITY WALLET

    Community wallet will be funded with 75% of the secondary sales. It will be used to invest back in the project to provide value to the holders. Community wallet will be made public so the community can easily follow how the funds are being utilised.
